(static) toHuman() → {String}

toHuman() generates a short version of the date and time, dependent on how long it is ago. Examples:

  1. In the future: "31. Dec 21:10"
  2. One day ago: "14:02"
  3. Earlier than one day ago, but this year: "14. Apr 15:00"
  4. Last year: "14. Apr 2018"
